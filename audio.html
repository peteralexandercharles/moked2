The Strong

In this piece you can see the combination of two different dramatic effects. Expressive clouds of a leaving storm behind the Strong Ship with unshakeable determination to survive. This is the opposition of two forces. It's difficult to go against heavy storms and thunderclouds of our life. We find in this Ship serenity, steadfastness and power of a champion.
